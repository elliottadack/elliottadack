The New Saga - Episode 1; The Return of Skywalker

As he emerged from the smoke, the vague glowing blade of his lightsaber humming in the mist, the leader of the Empire walked from an army of stormtroopers and resistance fighters. As the fighters droned in, explosions went off in the distance, smoke arising in the air. The distinct humm still remained as Jedi fought cautiosly in the background. For several weeks had this battle lasted, on the planet of Eurita.

3 Weeks Earlier...

"Lord. Our shield is locked down, for we shall not be able to prevent attack. We must react now." "Admiral, if i may insist, your plan to retalliate and cause a civil war is not of the Empire's best interest." "I advise you make cautious decisions if you are to invade the resistance." "Yes my Lord." The Admiral replied anxiously. "Fill me in on the girl." "Of course, Lord. Unfortunately, she refused to talk, regardless of our efforts. However, with her, she had a droid, enscripted with a message to the Resistance." "Admiral, I hope you did not attempt to read this message without consulting me." "Uhh, of course not, my Lord." The Admiral stutted nervously. "Then I best hope it is of great importance. Bring me the droid." "And the girl, Lord?" "Leave her to me."
